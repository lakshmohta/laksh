The Narrow Gauge Rail Museum is a unique museum at Kempty
Road, Nagpur. It serves as an information park, which provides
information on the functioning of the narrow gauge system and is
spread over an area of 5.6 acres. The museum is dedicated to
preserve the eventful history of the Indian Railways.

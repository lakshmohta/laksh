Pramukh Swami Maharaj sowed the seeds of satsang in 1976 when he visited the Vidarbha region. In
1993, Pramukh Swami Maharaj performed the murti-pratishtha of the hari mandir in the suburb of
Vardhmannagar. Thereafter, in 1997, Swamishri inaugurated The Swaminarayan School. In 2004, the
groundbreaking ceremony for the shikharbaddh mandir in the suburb of Wathoda was performed. The
murti-pratishtha of the newly constructed shikharbaddh mandir was performed in 2013.

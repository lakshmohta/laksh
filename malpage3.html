Futala Lake is a lake in Nagpur in the Indian state of Maharashtra. The lake
covers 60 acres (24 ha).[1] Built by the Bhosle kings of Nagpur, the lake is
known for its coloured fountains. In the evenings the site is illuminated with
halogen lights and Tanga (carriage) rides. The lake is surrounded on three
sides by forest and a landscaped beach on the fourth side.
